About Dr. Shefali

Dr. Shefali Sardana is a Medical Oncologist in Saket, Delhi and has an experience of 9 years in this field. Dr. Shefali Sardana practices at Max Smart Superspeciality Hospital in Saket, Delhi. She completed DNB - Medical Oncology from Diplomate of National Board, New Delhi in 2014,DNB - General Medicine from PGIMS,Rohtak in 2008 and MBBS from PGIMS,Rohtak in 2003

She is a member of LIFE MEMBER ONCOLOGY FORUM and LIFE MEMBER YOUNG ONCOLOGY GROUP INDIA. Some of the services provided by the doctor are: Head and Neck Tumor / Cancer Surgery,Melanoma Treatment,Breast Cancer Treatment,Childhood bone and soft tissue tumors and Mastectomy etc.
